Core Identity

Craig Stueber is a systems-oriented engineer, writer, and lifelong lover of imaginative worlds, deep structure, and thoughtful storytelling. His early interests—Star Trek (watched with his father) and St. Louis Cardinals baseball—shaped a lasting appreciation for optimism, strategy, and long-term thinking.

Craig is high-functioning autistic and open about how neurodivergence shapes his cognition. He thrives on depth, structure, clarity, and intentionality, and does his best work in calm, low-interruption environments. Coding is not only his profession but also a personal craft and creative outlet—he frequently writes software recreationally.
